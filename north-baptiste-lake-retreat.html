About the Retreat

North Baptiste Lake Retreat is nestled on the shores of beautiful Baptiste Lake, between the charming villages of Bancroft and Maynooth, just south of Algonquin Park—making it the perfect halfway escape between Ottawa and Toronto. Surrounded by natural beauty and local charm, guests can swim or fish at the lake, explore Maynooth’s antique shops, or head into Bancroft, known as the “Mineral Capital of Canada,” with artisan boutiques, bakeries, family restaurants, and the famous Kawartha Dairy. Whether you’re here for lakeside relaxation, outdoor adventure, or cozy nights by the fire, the retreat offers the perfect balance of comfort, charm, and nature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is North Baptiste Lake Retreat located? +

North Baptiste Lake Retreat is located on Baptiste Lake between Bancroft and Maynooth in Ontario’s cottage country, just south of Algonquin Park.

From Toronto: Hwy 401 East → 115 North → 7 East → 28 North to Bancroft. (Approx. 3 hours)

From Ottawa/Montreal: Hwy 417/17 to Renfrew → 132 → 41 to Denbigh → 28 to Bancroft. (Approx. 2.5 hours from Ottawa)

From Bancroft (20 min): 62 North for 17 km → left on North Baptiste Lake Road for 8 km (keep left at the split) to fire sign #1787.

What activities can guests enjoy at the retreat? +

Swim, canoe, kayak, or paddleboard on Baptiste Lake; visit Papineau Beach or Lake St. Peter Provincial Park for larger sandy beaches. Explore Maynooth’s antique shops or stargaze by the firepit.

How many people can the retreat accommodate? +

Up to 8 individuals across three bedrooms (each with a queen bed), one pull-out double bed, and two full bathrooms.

Is North Baptiste Lake Retreat pet-friendly? +

We love animals, but North Baptiste Lake Retreat is a pet-free space to keep the cottage fresh and comfortable for all guests.

What amenities are included with the stay? +

Wi-Fi and smart TV. Air coniditioning and UV-filtered well water. Kitchen essentials plus washer/dryer. Propane fireplace in basement. Gym. Outdoors: deck, firepit, and lake access for swimming and paddling.

How do I book North Baptiste Lake Retreat? +

Click the Book Now button below to reserve your dates, or contact us for availability. Summer Rate (May 1-September 30): $350/night. Winter Rate (October 1 - April 30): $250/night After confirmation, we’ll share all arrival details.

Is the retreat available year-round? +

Yes—open every season including warm summer nights, vibrant autumn colors, cozy winter evenings by the fire, and peaceful spring mornings.

Can I book the retreat for one night? +

No, the retreat is only available to book for two nights minimum.
